What our students say

Parent Review
Starting in Year 7

"My daughter joined the school six weeks ago in Year 7. I have seen a huge increase in her confidence and for the first time in about 2 years she doesn't complain about going to school in the morning. The teachers I have had contact with are lovely and really seem to care about the child as well as their education. Homework is achievable and the pupils can message teachers for help when needed. The reception staff and admin team are really helpful. I was so worried about how she would cope moving from a tiny primary into secondary (in an area she doesn't know and with no children from primary as we moved 30 miles away) bit I needn't have worried. I would recommend this school to anyone!"
